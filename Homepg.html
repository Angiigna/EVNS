Green Mapping

Why It Matters

Amid the hustle of academic life, an incredible world of life quietly flourishes at Pondicherry University. From towering trees to elusive birds and streetwise animals, nature weaves an unseen tapestry across our campus.

Through Green Mapping, we invite you to slow down, look closer, and reconnect with the living world that sustains and inspires us all.

The Living Canopy

Our campus blooms with rich tropical flora. From majestic rain trees to vibrant shrubs, each plant is a storyteller, silently shaping the ecosystem that supports countless lives. The campus landscape is not just beautiful—it's essential.

Whispers of the Crows

Agile and clever, the House Crows (Corvus splendens) have claimed Pondicherry University as their own. Their caws echo through the air, reminding us that wildlife thrives even in human spaces. Their presence weaves a connection between natural instincts and urban life.

Companions on Campus

With watchful eyes and quiet resilience, the campus dogs (Canis lupus familiaris) roam freely. They are companions, guardians, and survivors, adapting to the rhythms of university life and forming a unique bond with the students they share it with.

About Us

Our Mission

Pondicherry University Green Mapping is a passionate initiative to explore and document our campus's thriving biodiversity. Through storytelling, technology, and exploration, we aim to inspire awareness, respect, and responsibility toward the ecosystems that surround us.


By mapping plants, tracking wildlife, and uncovering hidden ecological patterns, we hope to create a living guide for students, researchers, and nature lovers alike.

Our Team

We are a vibrant group of 3rd-year Integrated Computer Science students, united by a shared passion for nature and technology. Together, we crafted this interactive journey through the green heart of Pondicherry University.
