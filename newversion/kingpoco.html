HONOURABLE MENTION
His Highness, Poco

In the annals of Pondicherry University, one name shall forever echo with loyalty, nobility, and love—Poco. A noble guardian, a silent sentry, and the softest of souls, His Highness reigned over our campus with dignity and unmatched devotion.

Be it under the starlit skies or sunlit streets, Poco walked not behind us—but beside us, as a friend, a protector, and a king in his own right. With every wag of his royal tail and every bark of warning, he reminded us that chivalry is not dead—and that sometimes, heroes have paws instead of swords.

Our King, Poco

Protector of Paths, Bringer of Peace, and the Cutest King of Campus.
