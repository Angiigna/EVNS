GREEN MAPPING

Why it matters?

Our campus is home to an incredible array of trees, shrubs, birds, and animals, many of which go unnoticed in the hustle of daily academic life. This project invites you to pause, observe, and learn about the ecological web that makes this university a truly green haven.

Flora of Pondicherry University: A Diverse and Vibrant Ecosystem.

Pondicherry University's campus is home to a diverse and vibrant array of flora, showcasing a wide variety of plants and trees that add to the beauty and biodiversity of the environment. The campus's flora is a testament to the region's tropical climate and rich ecological heritage.

House Crows in Pondicherry University: A Ubiquitous Presence

The House Crow (Corvus splendens) is a common and intelligent bird species found throughout Pondicherry University. These birds have adapted well to the urban environment and can be seen roaming around the campus, often in small groups. With their sleek black plumage and distinctive calls, House Crows are a familiar sight in the university.

Dogs in Pondicherry University: A Complex Relationship

Dogs (Canis lupus familiaris) are a common sight in Pondicherry University, roaming around the campus and interacting with students, faculty, and staff. These dogs have adapted to life on the streets, scavenging for food and avoiding hazards.

About Us

Our Mission

Pondicherry University Green Mapping is an initiative aimed at exploring and documenting the rich biodiversity of our campus, with a focus on both flora and fauna. Through interactive maps and data-driven storytelling, we strive to foster awareness, curiosity, and responsibility toward our natural surroundings.


By pinpointing plant species, tracking animal movements, and highlighting behavioral patterns, this platform seeks to provide an educational resource for students, researchers, and nature enthusiasts alike.

Worked together

We are vibrant enthusiastic students of 3rd-year Integrated Computer Science department projects green mapping the rich ecological diversity of Pondicherry University Campus through an interactive website.
